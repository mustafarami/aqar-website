Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Aqar عقار ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Aqar:

  • Email address and display name (when you register)
  • Listing information you provide (title, description, price, location, photos)
  • Messages you send through the in-app chat
  • Device information and push notification tokens
  • Photos you upload from your device

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the Aqar marketplace
  • Display your listings to other users
  • Enable real-time messaging between buyers and sellers
  • Send push notifications about your listings and messages
  • Improve the app experience

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in these cases:

  • Listing information is visible to all users of the app
  • Messages are only visible to the participants of that conversation
  • We use Firebase (Google) for authentication and database storage
  • We use Cloudinary for photo storage

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your listings at any time from within the app. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the email below.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account

6. Children's Privacy

Aqar is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
